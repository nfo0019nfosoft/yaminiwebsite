About Yamini MultiSpeciality Hospital

Committed to Excellence in Women, Children & Orthopaedic Care

Who We Are

Yamini MultiSpeciality Hospital is a trusted centre of excellence for Paediatrics, Neonatology, Orthopaedics, Gynaecology and Mother–Child Care. With expert doctors, advanced facilities, and 24/7 emergency services, we are committed to providing safe, high-quality and compassionate healthcare.

Our mission is to make world-class medical care accessible and affordable for every patient, ensuring comfort, trust and healing at every step.

Our Mission

To deliver modern, compassionate and affordable healthcare with excellence and integrity.

Our Vision

To be the region’s most trusted hospital in Mother & Child care, Orthopaedics and Women’s Wellness.

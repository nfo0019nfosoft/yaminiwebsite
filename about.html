About Yamini MultiSpeciality Hospital

Committed to Excellence in Women, Children & Orthopaedic Care

WELCOME TO YAMINI HOSPITAL

Yamini MultiSpeciality Hospital is a trusted centre of excellence for Paediatrics, Neonatology, Orthopaedics, Gynaecology, and comprehensive Mother–Child Care. With a team of highly experienced doctors, modern medical technology, and well-equipped treatment facilities, we ensure accurate diagnosis and effective care for every patient. Our hospital provides 24/7 emergency services, advanced intensive care units, and round-the-clock medical support to handle critical situations with confidence and speed. Our mission is to promote healthier lives through continuous innovation, preventive care, and community trust—making Yamini MultiSpeciality Hospital a reliable healthcare partner for every stage of life.We are committed to patient-centred care, focusing on safety, compassion, and ethical medical practices. From routine consultations to complex procedures, our multidisciplinary team works together to deliver personalized treatment plans and the highest standards of healthcare for children, women, and families.

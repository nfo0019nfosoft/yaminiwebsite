Paediatrics & Neonatology Services

Seizures, Headaches & Weakness

Seizures, severe headaches, and sudden weakness can indicate neurological issues. Causes include fever, infection, migraines, or metabolic disturbances. Early evaluation, imaging and treatment prevent complications and protect development. A headache is a common condition characterized by pain in the head, face, or neck region. They are one of the most common medical complaints and can range from a mild annoyance to a debilitating pain.

Vaccination

Daily vaccinations following IAP schedules. Digital immunization records, guidance on vaccine timing, and safe administration.Vaccination is a safe and effective way to protect individuals and communities from serious, life-threatening diseases by building immunity against specific viruses and bacteria. Health authorities like the World Health Organization (WHO) and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) recommend specific vaccination schedules for all ages

Growth & Development

Monitoring height, weight, speech, motor and social milestones. Early identification of delays ensures timely therapy and healthy development.Growth and development are two interconnected processes that describe the continuous physical and functional changes an individual undergoes throughout their lifespan. While the terms are often used together, they refer to distinct aspects of maturation

Fever, Cold, Cough & Flu

Expert diagnosis of viral/bacterial infections, safe medication, hydration care, and prevention guidance. Follow-up ensures quick recovery and reduces complications.Fever, cough, and cold are common symptoms that often occur together, typically indicating a respiratory infection caused by a virus. These are the body's natural defense mechanisms working to fight off the illness.

Asthma & Respiratory Problems

Nebulization, inhaler training, allergy testing and long-term asthma plans. Acute attacks managed promptly; chronic care prevents recurrence.Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition in which the airways (bronchial tubes) narrow, swell, and produce extra mucus, making breathing difficult and triggering coughing, wheezing, and shortness of breath. It is a long-term condition that requires ongoing management

Paediatrics & Neonatology Services

NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care)

Our NICU provides advanced medical support for premature and critically ill newborns who need continuous monitoring and specialised care. The controlled environment maintains ideal warmth and breathing support. Nutrition is supervised to promote healthy development. Expert neonatologists monitor babies round-the-clock. This ensures a safe and stable beginning for newborns during their most delicate phase.

PICU (Paediatric Intensive Care)

The PICU provides specialised care for critically ill infants and children requiring emergency stabilisation and life-support. Advanced equipment supports respiratory, cardiac and neurological conditions. A trained paediatric team monitors children continuously to ensure timely interventions. The goal is to stabilise and support recovery. This intensive care system greatly enhances the chances of safe healing.

New-born Care

Newborn care includes routine health checks, feeding assessment, jaundice screening and monitoring of early growth patterns. Parents receive guidance on safe newborn handling and breastfeeding. Early detection of problems ensures timely treatment. Our team supports a smooth transition from birth to home. This ensures a strong foundation for lifelong health.

Jaundice Treatment

Jaundice is treated using safe, advanced phototherapy to gradually reduce bilirubin levels. Babies are monitored closely to ensure steady improvement. Hydration and feeding support help enhance recovery. Early care prevents long-term complications. The treatment environment is calm, safe and designed for newborn comfort.

Preterm Baby Care

Preterm babies need specialised support because their organs are still developing. We provide respiratory assistance, feeding management and thermal protection. Infection prevention is a major priority. Neonatologists adjust care plans based on each infant's needs. This approach helps premature babies grow stronger and healthier each day.

Vaccination

Vaccination plays a vital role in protecting children from life-threatening diseases. We follow national immunisation schedules and maintain proper records for parents. The procedures are carried out safely in a hygienic environment. Timely reminders ensure no dose is missed. Immunisation helps build strong, long-lasting immunity for every child.

Fever, Cold, Cough & Flu

Common infections such as fever, cold and cough are evaluated to determine whether they are viral or bacterial. Treatment focuses on comfort, hydration and safe medication. Early care prevents severe complications. Parents receive guidance for home care. With proper monitoring, children recover comfortably and quickly.

Asthma & Respiratory Problems

Respiratory issues like asthma and wheezing require structured long-term management. We provide nebulisation, inhaler training and trigger identification. Regular follow-up helps reduce flare- ups. Parents are taught preventive measures. Our aim is to help children breathe freely and stay active with confidence.

Skin Allergy & Rashes

Skin allergies and rashes often result from infections, food reactions or environmental triggers. We identify the exact cause and provide soothing, effective treatment. Skin care routines are recommended to prevent flare-ups. With proper guidance, children experience long-term comfort. Healthy skin is restored safely.

Stomach Pain, Diarrhea & Vomiting

Digestive problems in children may occur due to infections, food intolerance or dehydration. We assess hydration levels and provide appropriate fluid therapy. Early diagnosis ensures fast relief. Parents receive guidance on diet and care. With timely treatment, children regain strength quickly and safely.

Ear, Nose & Throat Infections

ENT infections affect breathing, feeding and sleep in children. We diagnose problems like ear pain, sinus congestion and tonsil inflammation. Treatment reduces discomfort quickly. Follow-up care helps prevent recurrent infections. Our goal is healthier breathing and faster recovery.

Growth & Development

Tracking a child’s milestones helps identify growth progress and potential delays early. We monitor height, weight, learning skills and behaviour. Nutrition and lifestyle suggestions are given to support healthy development. Early intervention ensures every child reaches their potential. This holistic monitoring builds lifelong well-being.

Seizures, Headaches & Weakness

Neurological symptoms such as seizures and severe headaches need immediate medical attention. We conduct detailed neurological assessments and investigations when required. Early diagnosis prevents serious complications. Treatment plans are tailored to each child’s condition. Continuous monitoring ensures long-term brain health and stability.

Urinary & Kidney Problems

Urinary infections, kidney issues and bladder problems are evaluated carefully to avoid long-term damage. Children receive gentle, effective treatment. Hygiene and hydration guidance is given to parents. Follow-up ensures complete recovery. Our focus is on maintaining healthy urinary system function.

General Paediatric Surgeries

Common childhood surgeries are performed by experienced paediatric surgeons in a safe, child-friendly environment. Anaesthesia and postoperative care are managed carefully to ensure comfort. Healing is monitored until complete recovery. Our focus is on safety, precision and compassionate care for every child.

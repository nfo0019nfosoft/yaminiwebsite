Gynaecology & Obstetrics Services

Menstrual Disorders Treatment

Treatment for irregular cycles, heavy bleeding and severe cramps.Menstrual disorders are a wide range of conditions that involve problems with a woman's normal menstrual cycle, including irregular periods, heavy bleeding, painful menstruation, or absent periods Includes ultrasound evaluation & hormone testing. Personalized medication and lifestyle correction. Focus on restoring pain-free and regular menstruation.

PCOS / PCOD Management

Comprehensive evaluation of hormonal imbalance and metabolic issues.PCOD (Polycystic Ovarian Disease) and PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) are both hormonal conditions affecting women, but they differ in severity and scope. PCOD is a more common, generally milder condition focused on ovarian dysfunction, while PCOS is a complex, serious metabolic and endocrine syndrome with broader long-term health implications Helps manage irregular cycles, acne, hair growth & weight issues. Includes tailored medications, diet and lifestyle plans. Supports long-term hormonal balance & fertility.

Thyroid & High Blood Pressure

Evaluation & management of thyroid disorders in women.Blood pressure (BP) is the force of blood pushing against the walls of your arteries as your heart pumps blood throughout your body. It is a vital sign that indicates the efficiency of your cardiovascular system Treatment for pregnancy-related BP and long-term hypertension. Prevents complications for both mother and baby.High blood pressure (hypertension) is often called the "silent killer" because most people experience no symptoms, even at dangerously high levels. Regular monitoring ensures safe outcomes.

Vaginal Infections / UTI Treatment

Treatment for itching, discharge and burning urination.vaginal relates to the vagina, which is an elastic, muscular canal that is an essential part of the female reproductive tract. It serves multiple critical biological functions throughout a woman's life. Lab tests ensure accurate diagnosis. Proper medication & hygiene guidance.Vaginal delivery offers numerous advantages for both the mother and the baby Restores comfort and long-term vaginal health.

Normal Delivery

Safe natural birthing with continuous monitoring. Experienced obstetric team ensures comfort & safety. Pain-relief options and birthing guidance available. Focus on healthy labour & joyful childbirth. A normal delivery, medically known as a vaginal birth or spontaneous vaginal delivery (SVD), is the natural process of giving birth through the birth canal (vagina) without surgical intervention like a C-section. It is considered the optimal and safest method of childbirth for most pregnancies

Cesarean Section (C-Section)

A Cesarean section (C-section) is a surgical procedure used to deliver a baby through incisions made in the mother's abdomen and uterus, rather than through the vagina. It is a common procedure that can be planned in advance or performed as an emergency based on medical necessity.Safe surgical delivery when medically needed. Performed in a sterile, modern operating theatre. Quick-recovery protocols and post-operative care included Ensures a safe birth in emergency & high-risk cases.

Gynaecology & Obstetrics Services

Menstrual Disorders Treatment

Menstrual disorders include irregular cycles, heavy bleeding and severe cramps that affect daily life. Ultrasound and hormone evaluation help identify underlying causes like PCOS or thyroid imbalance. Treatment is customized to correct cycle irregularity and reduce discomfort. Nutritional and lifestyle counseling support long-term hormonal stability. Medications help regulate the menstrual cycle safely. Early diagnosis prevents complications such as anemia and fertility problems. Our goal is restoring a healthy, predictable menstrual cycle for overall well-being.

PCOS / PCOD Management

PCOS causes irregular periods, weight gain, acne and hormonal imbalance in many women. Diagnosis involves hormonal profiling and pelvic ultrasound. Treatment focuses on restoring ovulation and cycle regularity. Lifestyle changes such as diet and exercise greatly improve hormonal health. Medications help balance insulin levels and regulate periods. Early management prevents long-term risks like diabetes and infertility. Continuous monitoring ensures steady improvement and better reproductive outcomes.

Thyroid & High Blood Pressure

Thyroid disorders can lead to fatigue, weight changes and menstrual problems. BP issues affect pregnancy and overall health. We perform detailed hormonal tests and regular BP monitoring. Treatment includes safe medications and lifestyle modifications. Early control prevents complications during pregnancy. Ongoing follow-up ensures stable health. Proper management restores hormonal balance and improves energy.

White Discharge Treatment

Abnormal discharge may indicate infections, hormonal issues or pelvic conditions. We perform examination and necessary lab tests to detect the cause. Treatment includes antibiotics or antifungal medications. Hygiene guidance prevents recurrence and irritation. Early care avoids spread to the uterus or tubes. Patients receive supportive counseling for comfort and confidence. Our aim is restoring normal, healthy vaginal function safely.

Uterine Fibroids

Fibroids are non-cancerous growths that cause heavy bleeding, pelvic pain and infertility. Diagnosis is done through ultrasound and medical evaluation. Treatment ranges from medication to minimally invasive surgery depending on size and symptoms. Managing fibroids early prevents anemia and pregnancy issues. Regular monitoring ensures they don’t grow or return. Our treatment focuses on long-term comfort and preserving fertility whenever possible.

Ovarian Cyst Diagnosis & Treatment

Ovarian cysts can cause pelvic pain, delayed periods and hormonal fluctuations. We use ultrasound to evaluate their size and type. Small cysts often resolve with medication and monitoring. Larger or painful cysts may require laparoscopy for safe removal. Early treatment avoids complications like rupture or torsion. Our care plan protects ovarian function and future fertility.

Endometriosis

Endometriosis causes severe menstrual pain, infertility and chronic pelvic discomfort. Diagnosis requires medical history, ultrasound and sometimes laparoscopy. Treatment includes hormonal therapies to reduce pain and inflammation. Surgical correction may be needed for severe cases. Early care improves fertility chances and reduces long-term complications. We provide continuous support for symptom management and overall well-being.

Pelvic Pain Evaluation

Pelvic pain can arise from infections, cysts, uterus conditions or intestinal issues. We conduct a thorough evaluation including scans and lab tests. Treatment targets the root cause to ensure proper recovery. Pain relief measures help improve daily function. Early diagnosis prevents chronic conditions. Guidance is provided on lifestyle, posture and reproductive care. Our approach ensures safe and complete healing.

Cervical Screening (Pap Smear)

A Pap smear detects early cell changes that can lead to cervical cancer. The test is quick, painless and highly effective. Regular screening helps catch abnormalities early for timely treatment. HPV testing may be advised alongside Pap tests. Early detection greatly improves treatment outcomes. We ensure a safe, comfortable screening experience. Essential for every woman’s reproductive health.

Vaginal Infections / UTI Treatment

Vaginal infections cause itching, odor, burning and discharge, while UTIs affect urinary comfort. Diagnosis is done through swab tests and urine analysis. Treatment includes antibiotics or antifungal medication. Proper hygiene and hydration reduce recurrence risk. Ignoring symptoms may lead to kidney infections. Early care restores comfort and prevents complications. Our approach ensures fast relief and long-term protection.

Menopause Management

Menopause causes hot flashes, sleep issues, mood swings and bone weakness. Hormone evaluation helps guide safe treatment options. Lifestyle modifications improve comfort significantly. Supplements may be recommended for bone protection. Regular monitoring prevents long-term effects like osteoporosis. Compassionate care helps women adjust with confidence. We support physical and emotional well-being.

High-Risk Pregnancy Care

High-risk pregnancies require close monitoring to protect mother and baby. Conditions like hypertension, diabetes and thyroid disorders are managed with expert care. Frequent scans ensure normal baby growth. Early detection of complications allows timely interventions. A specialized team is available 24/7 for emergencies. Personalized pregnancy plans ensure safety. Our focus is a healthy and successful delivery.

Normal Delivery

Normal delivery is encouraged whenever safe for mother and baby. Continuous monitoring helps identify progress and ensure safety. Pain-relief options are available for comfort. Skilled guidance supports smooth labour. Faster postpartum recovery is a key advantage. Mother and baby bonding begins early. Our team ensures a safe and positive birthing experience.

Cesarean Section (C-Section)

A C-section is performed when normal delivery is unsafe for mother or baby. Our surgical team ensures a controlled, safe procedure with minimal discomfort. Pain management improves early mobility and recovery. Follow-up care focuses on wound healing and breastfeeding support. This procedure prevents complications in high-risk pregnancies. The goal is delivering a healthy baby safely. We guide mothers through every step with compassion.

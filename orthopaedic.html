Orthopaedic Services

Trauma Management

Immediate stabilization of fractures and soft-tissue injuries.Emergency wound care, splinting & casting.Trauma management refers to the critical, systematic approach to both physical injuries resulting from sudden, forceful events and the psychological impact of highly stressful experiences.Team approach with anaesthesia & physiotherapy.Early rehabilitation improves recovery and function.

Total Knee Replacement (TKR)

Advanced joint replacement for severe knee arthritis.Minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery.Early physiotherapy for mobility & stability.Long-term pain relief and improved walking ability.Total knee replacement (TKR), also called total knee arthroplasty, is a surgical procedure that resurfaces a severely damaged knee joint with artificial metal and plastic components to relieve chronic pain and restore mobility. It is one of the most successful and cost-effective procedures in orthopedics, offering significant improvements in quality of life for suitable candidates.

Total Hip Replacement (THR)

Restores hip function for arthritis or fracture complications.Precision surgical planning and safe anaesthesia.Enhanced recovery pathways reduce hospital stay.Physiotherapy supports strength and proper gait.Total hip replacement (THR), or total hip arthroplasty, is a major surgical procedure where the damaged ball-and-socket joint of the hip is removed and replaced with artificial prosthetic components made of metal, plastic, or ceramic. This operation is highly effective at relieving chronic pain, stiffness, and restoring normal movement in people whose hip joints are severely degraded.

Shoulder Joint Replacement

Treatment for severe arthritis & complex fractures.Includes both anatomic & reverse replacements.Surgery restores shoulder function & reduces pain.Structured rehabilitation improves motion & strength.Total shoulder replacement, or shoulder arthroplasty, is a surgical procedure to replace a damaged shoulder joint with artificial components to relieve severe pain and restore motion. The procedure has become highly successful in managing chronic pain and disability when non-surgical treatments fail.

Joint Pain Evaluation

Joint pain replacement refers to a surgical solution for chronic and severe joint pain where the damaged joint is removed and replaced with an artificial implant (prosthesis). This procedure is considered when non-surgical treatments fail to provide reliefAssessment of knee, hip, shoulder & ankle pain.X-ray, MRI & ultrasound for accurate diagnosis.Non-surgical treatment options available.Goal is pain relief & restoring joint function.

Back & Neck Pain Treatment

Back and neck pain are common conditions typically caused by muscle strain, poor posture, or age-related degeneration, although serious underlying issues are possible. Management often involves self-care, physical therapy, and medication, while urgent symptoms require immediate medical evaluation.Evidence-based treatment for spine pain & nerve compression.Physiotherapy, pain procedures & lifestyle modifications.Minimally invasive spine surgery for select cases.Rehab includes posture correction & core strengthening.

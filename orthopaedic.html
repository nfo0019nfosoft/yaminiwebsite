Orthopaedic Services

Trauma Management

Comprehensive care for road accidents, falls and severe injuries requiring immediate attention. Includes fracture stabilisation, wound care, plastering and emergency pain control. Team approach ensures safe and fast recovery. Early intervention reduces long-term complications. Treatment is tailored to restore mobility quickly and safely. Trauma care ensures immediate stabilisation, preventing long-term disability. Specialised protocols help restore full function with minimal complications.

Total Knee Replacement (TKR)

TKR surgery restores knee function in patients with severe arthritis or joint damage. The worn-out surfaces are replaced with artificial implants. This reduces pain, improves walking ability and enhances daily movement. Modern techniques ensure faster recovery. Ideal for long-standing knee pain. Most patients regain excellent walking ability within weeks of surgery. Modern implants are highly durable and support active lifestyles.

Total Hip Replacement (THR)

THR surgery replaces the damaged hip joint with a smooth artificial implant. It relieves severe hip pain caused by arthritis, fractures or wear-and-tear. Patients experience improved flexibility and mobility. Modern hip implants last many years. Recovery allows patients to return to an active life. Hip replacement reduces stiffness and restores smooth, pain-free movement. Patients often experience major improvement in sitting, standing and mobility.

Shoulder Joint Replacement

Shoulder replacement helps patients with severe arthritis, rotator cuff damage or joint injuries. The damaged bone is replaced with a smooth artificial joint. This reduces pain and improves arm movement. Ideal for patients who struggle with lifting or daily shoulder use. Offers long-term relief. The procedure significantly improves shoulder rotation and strength. Daily tasks like lifting, combing hair and reaching become easier.

Fracture Care & Trauma Management

Complete treatment for all types of fractures, from simple cracks to complex multiple-bone injuries. Includes plastering, splints, minimally invasive surgery and rehabilitation. Ensures proper bone alignment and healing. Early mobilisation is encouraged for better recovery. Goal is to restore normal movement. Precise alignment techniques ensure natural bone healing. Rehabilitation support helps patients regain strength quickly.

Arthritis Diagnosis & Treatment

Arthritis leads to joint pain, swelling and stiffness that can affect daily activities. We offer medication, physiotherapy, injections and lifestyle guidance. Early diagnosis helps slow disease progression. Personalized treatment reduces pain and improves mobility. Long-term management enhances quality of life. Regular monitoring prevents worsening of joint damage. Guided exercise therapy improves long-term flexibility.

Sports Injuries Management

Specialised care for ligament tears, muscle sprains, tendon injuries and joint trauma. Treatment includes physiotherapy, bracing and minimally invasive procedures. Focus on restoring full strength and movement. Preventive guidance avoids repeat injuries. Helps athletes return to sports safely and confidently. Rehabilitation programs are customised for each sport. Strength training prevents future ligament damage.

Joint Pain Evaluation

Detailed assessment for knee, hip, shoulder and ankle pain. Evaluation includes X-rays, gait analysis and clinical examination. Identifies arthritis, injuries or alignment issues. Treatment improves movement and reduces pain. Helps prevent long-term joint deterioration. Early detection reduces the risk of severe degeneration. Personalized treatment plans ensure faster recovery.

Disc Problems / Back Pain

Disc bulges and herniations can cause nerve compression and radiating pain. Treatment includes medication, physiotherapy and strengthening exercises. Minimally invasive procedures may be recommended in severe cases. Early care prevents worsening symptoms. Goal is pain-free mobility. Strengthening the core reduces disc pressure effectively. Early treatment helps avoid the need for surgery.

Spine Fracture Management

Spine fractures require urgent stabilisation to prevent nerve damage. We evaluate the severity using imaging studies and provide appropriate bracing or surgical correction. Rehabilitation restores strength and mobility. Focus is on protecting spinal cord function. Ensures safe healing. Advanced stabilisation techniques prevent deformity. Careful monitoring improves long-term spinal health.

Ankle Ligament Injuries

Sudden twists and sports activity commonly injure ankle ligaments. Treatment includes rest, physiotherapy, bracing and strengthening exercises. Severe cases may require reconstruction. Early care prevents chronic instability. Helps patients return to normal activities safely. Proper rehab prevents long-term ankle weakness. Balance training reduces chances of re-injury.

Shoulder Dislocations

Shoulder dislocations cause severe pain and instability. We reposition the joint safely and evaluate for ligament tears. Physiotherapy helps restore strength. Recurrent dislocations may need minimally invasive surgery. Prompt care ensures proper healing and stable shoulder function. Strengthening the rotator cuff prevents future dislocations. Proper rehabilitation restores complete joint stability.

Wrist & Elbow Disorders

Pain, stiffness or swelling in the wrist and elbow can result from injuries, arthritis or nerve compression. We diagnose the issue using imaging and functional tests. Treatment includes therapy, medication and splinting. Proper care ensures restored joint movement. Helps prevent future problems. Early diagnosis avoids long-term stiffness. Strengthening exercises improve daily hand function.

Tendon, Ligament & Muscle Injury Care

Strains, tears and inflammation of tendons or muscles can affect movement and cause pain. We offer rest protocols, physiotherapy, medication and advanced treatments when required. Strengthening exercises help prevent recurrence. Recovery is monitored closely. Designed to restore strength and flexibility. Therapy improves flexibility and muscle coordination. Follow-up ensures safe and complete healing.

Osteoporosis Screening & Treatment

Osteoporosis weakens bones and increases fracture risk. We perform bone density testing to detect early bone loss. Treatment includes supplements, medication and lifestyle guidance. Weight-bearing exercises strengthen bones. Early screening ensures long-term bone health and fracture prevention. Timely treatment reduces fracture chances drastically. Lifestyle changes improve lifelong bone strength.

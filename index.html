DR. SIRISHRTTI YAMINI KUMARI
MBBS, MD (PAEDIATRICS), FIAP
Consultant Paediatrician Neonatologist New-born & Preterm Care
About Doctor

Dr. Sirishrtti Yamini Kumari is a consultant paediatrician and neonatologist with experience in managing new-borns, preterm babies and complex neonatal conditions. Completed FIAP fellowship in Neonatology from Rainbow Children’s Hospital with a focus on intensive care and holistic child health.

DR. B. NARASMHA JEEVAN KUMAR

Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon Joint Replacement Specialist Sports & Trauma Care
About Doctor

Dr. B. Narasmha Jeevan Kumar is an orthopaedic and joint replacement surgeon with expertise in managing fractures, arthritis and complex joint problems. Trained in FIJR (Ahmedabad), he focuses on painless movement, faster recovery and personalised rehabilitation for every patient.

General Medicine Services

All Types of Fever

Comprehensive evaluation for viral, bacterial and seasonal fevers affecting children and adults. Diagnosis includes temperature monitoring, blood tests and infection screening. Early treatment prevents dehydration and complications. Symptomatic relief and antibiotic care when required. Lifestyle and hygiene advice helps prevent recurrence. Focus on complete and safe recovery. Fever can be categorized by its duration, temperature range, or the pattern of temperature changes over time. Understanding the type of fever can help healthcare providers determine the underlying cause and appropriate treatment

Diabetes

Management of Type 1, Type 2 and gestational diabetes with modern treatment guidelines. Blood sugar monitoring and lifestyle modifications with customized diet plans. Medications and insulin therapy based on individual needs. Preventive care for kidney, nerve and eye complications. Monthly follow-up ensures controlled blood sugar levels. Diabetes mellitus is a chronic condition where blood glucose (sugar) levels are too high because the body either doesn't produce enough insulin, or cannot effectively use the insulin it produces. Insulin is a hormone that regulates blood sugar by helping glucose enter the body's cells for energy

Hypertension

Accurate BP diagnosis with lifestyle and medication-based treatment. Routine monitoring to avoid stroke, heart disease and kidney damage. Personalized diet and exercise guidance. Stress management support for long-term BP control. Early treatment ensures safe and stable cardiovascular health.Hypertension, or high blood pressure, is a common chronic medical condition where the force of blood pushing against the artery walls is persistently too high. It is often called the "silent killer" because it typically has no obvious symptoms until severe complications arise, such as heart attack or stroke

Thyroid Problems

Diagnosis and management of hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism. Hormone testing and long-term treatment for stable thyroid levels. Guidance on fatigue, weight changes and mood symptoms. Regular follow-up ensures balance and healthy metabolism. Focus on improving daily energy levels and wellbeing.The thyroid is a small, butterfly-shaped gland located in the front of the neck that produces hormones essential for regulating the body's metabolism, growth, development, and energy use.

Respiratory Infections

Treatment for cold, cough, bronchitis, pneumonia and asthma-related infections. Nebulization and inhalation therapy offered when required. Early diagnosis prevents severe breathing issues. Antibiotics or antiviral medicines based on condition. Proper care ensures quick and safe recovery.Respiratory infections are illnesses that affect the parts of the body involved in breathing, including the nose, throat, airways, and lungs. They are typically categorized into upper and lower respiratory tract infections, with causes ranging from viruses and bacteria to fungi

Urinary Infection

Diagnosis and treatment of UTI with urine tests and medical evaluation. Relieves burning, frequent urination and abdominal discomfort. Strong focus on preventing kidney-related complications. Hydration and hygiene guidance included. Fast-relief medication ensures comfortable recovery and long-term protection.A urinary tract infection (UTI) is an infection of any part of the urinary system, which includes the kidneys, ureters, bladder, and urethra. UTIs are typically caused by bacteria, most often E. coli from the gastrointestinal (GI) tract, entering the urinary tract through the urethra and multiplying

Gastro Infections

Management of stomach infections, diarrhea, vomiting and food poisoning. Fluid and electrolyte balancing to avoid dehydration. Medication for infection control and digestion support. Quick diagnosis helps prevent complications. Diet guidance improves gut recovery.A gastrointestinal (GI) infection, commonly known as the "stomach flu" or gastroenteritis, is an inflammation of the lining of the stomach and intestines. These infections cause symptoms like diarrhea, vomiting, and abdominal pain and are usually caused by viruses, bacteria, or parasites

Neuro Problems

Evaluation and treatment of headaches, dizziness, nerve pain and neurological issues. Neurological examination and imaging when required. Early detection avoids long-term nerve complications. Medication and lifestyle support provided. Focus on improving mobility, balance and daily function.Neurological problems refer to a broad range of conditions affecting the nervous system—the brain, spinal cord, and the vast network of nerves throughout the body. These disorders can disrupt how you move, feel, think, and communicate

Joint Pains

Treatment for knee, hip, shoulder and generalized joint pain. Evaluation includes arthritis screening and mobility analysis. Medication, physiotherapy and lifestyle correction advised. Early treatment prevents stiffness and long-term damage. Focus on painless movement and improved daily activity.Joint pain, or arthralgia, is a common issue that can range from a mild ache to severe discomfort that limits movement. It affects one or more joints and can be caused by injuries, infections, or underlying chronic medical conditions like arthritis.

Migraine

Specialized care for migraine and severe headaches. Identification of triggers such as stress, sleep and diet. Preventive and quick-relief medications provided. Relaxation and lifestyle guidance reduce attack frequency. Aim is long-term relief and improved quality of life.Migraine is a complex neurological condition that is much more than just a bad headache; it is a disease of abnormal brain function. It is a primary cause of disability worldwide and is characterized by recurrent attacks that can be debilitating

Kidney Diseases

Diagnosis and management of kidney infections, stones and early kidney failure. Blood and urine tests help detect function changes. Diet and hydration plans support kidney recovery. Preventive care avoids further damage. Regular monitoring ensures long-term kidney health and stability.Kidney disease means the kidneys are damaged and cannot filter blood properly, leading to a buildup of waste and extra fluid in the body. It is a serious condition that can be managed, but often without a cure for the most common type

Heart Diseases

Screening and treatment for chest pain, palpitations, high BP and early heart disease. ECG, echo and blood tests help accurate diagnosis. Medication stabilizes heart function. Lifestyle, diet and exercise guidance reduce risk. Early care prevents long-term cardiac complications. Focus on a healthy and active life.Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the function and structure of the heart and blood vessels. It is a leading cause of death worldwide, but many types can be managed or prevented with lifestyle changes and medical care
